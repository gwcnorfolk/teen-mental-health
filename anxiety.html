What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is when you feel surrounded with fear; you cannot escape it, you feel trapped, and you are desprate to break free of this feeling.

Image by ElisaRiva from Pixabay

Am I the only person feeling this way?

We all have anxiety. The only difference is that people react to it differently. Some can control it and others cannot. You are not alone if you cannot. Many people suffer with this and they too have trouble controlling it.

Image by Thank you for your support Donations welcome to support from Pixabay

How can I manage this?

If you start to feel anxious, you can relieve it in many ways. One way in particular is to take deep breaths and try to avoid thinking about what is making you feel this way. Other ways invole talking to a friend, family member, or a trusted person and express how and why you are feeling like this. If talking does not help, writing in a journal can improve your ability to control your emotions and can reduce, prevent, and make your anxiousness disappear.

I want to learn more, where do I go?

If none of these methods have worked, you can take a look at provided website links and explore more in depth of why you may have anxiety and what you can do to reduce the awful feeling it brings.
