Questions and Answers about Anxiety

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is when you feel surrounded by fear; you cannot escape it, you feel trapped, and you are desperate to break free of this feeling.

Image by ElisaRiva from Pixabay

Am I the only person feeling this way?

We all have anxiety. The only difference is that people react to it differently. Some can control it and others cannot. You are not alone if you cannot. Many people suffer from this and they too have trouble controlling it.

Image by Thank you for your support Donations welcome to support from Pixabay

How can I manage this?

If you start to feel anxious, you can relieve it in many ways. One way, in particular, is to take deep breaths and try the anxiety calming exercises in this app! Other ways involve talking to a friend, family member, or a trusted person and express how and why you are feeling like this. If talking does not help, writing in a journal can improve your ability to control your emotions and can reduce, prevent, and make your anxiousness disappear.

I want to learn more, where do I go?

If none of these methods have worked, you can take a look at our resource page links and explore more in-depth why you may have anxiety and what you can do to reduce the awful feeling it brings.
